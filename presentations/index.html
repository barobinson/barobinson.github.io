Presentations

Here you can see a list of talks and posters which I have presented, with links to abstracts and PDFs where available.

Optimal Control of Martingales in a Radially Symmetric Environment

April 2019, Contributed Talk

  • Talk (PDF) at the 2nd Leeds Conference on Stochastic Control and Games Under Ambiguity, University of Leeds, on 9th April 2019.

When Should I Stop? An Introduction to Optimal Stopping

October 2018, Seminar

  • Talk at the Postgraduate Seminar Series, University of Bath, on 11th October 2018, introducing optimal stopping, via an interactive demonstration of the classical "Secretary Problem".
    Download code (iPython Notebook) to simulate the optimal stopping rule in this problem.

Introduction to Stochastic Optimal Control

March 2018, Seminar

  • Talk at the Student Probability Seminar, University of Bath, on 20th March 2018, presenting some of the basic ideas in stochastic optimal control .

Introduction to Viscosity Solutions

November 2017, Seminar

  • Talk at the Postgraduate Mathematical Analysis Seminar, University of Bath, on 27th November 2017, introducing the notion of viscosity solutions to second order elliptic PDEs.

How to Move a Pile of Sand: An Introduction to Optimal Transport

October 2017, Seminar

  • Talk at the Postgraduate Seminar Series, University of Bath, on 5th October 2017, intoducing the Monge and Kantorovich formulations of optimal transport.

Stochastic Optimal Control Problems Related to Martingale Optimal Transport

June & September 2017, Talk and Poster

  • Short talk at BUC 8 at CIMAT (Guanajuato, Mexico) on 1st June 2017, outlining the class of stochastic optimal control problems I am studying, methods to approach these problems, and links to martingale optimal transport.

  • Poster (PDF) presented at the Faculty of Science Graduate School Research Afternoon on 12th June 2017 and the SAMBa Summer Conference on 20th June 2017, both at the University of Bath.

  • Poster (PDF) presented at the Conference on Stochastic Control, Ambiguity and Games on 4th September 2017, at the University of Leeds.

Random Motion and PDEs: What's the Connection?

February 2017, Seminar

  • Talk at the Postgraduate Seminar Series, University of Bath, on 23rd February 2017, introducing Brownian motion and describing the stochastic respresentation of solutions to some elliptic and parabolic PDEs.

Sea Ice: Data, Modelling and Uncertainty

February 2016, Seminar

  • Talk (PDF) at the Postgraduate Seminar Series, University of Bath, on 25th February 2018, describing work undertaken at SAMBa ITT3 to model historical sea ice levels in collaboration with the Met Office Hadley Centre.
